What is it made of?

  • Glass: Smooth and durable surface for the display
  • Liquid crystals: Contort with electric current
  • LED Backlight: Semiconductors (GaAs, GaP)
  • Plastic: External frame and casing
  • Metals: Structural component, electronics
  • Electronics: ICs, transistors, capacitors, resistors
  • Adhesives/Sealants: Bond layers of LCD screen
  • Inorganic: ITO for transparent conductive coatings
